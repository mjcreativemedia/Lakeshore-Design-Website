What this is

Lakeshore Local is a hands-on beta for brick-and-mortar teams who want marketing that feels like their block. We blend brand, copy, and lightweight tech to launch campaigns that match your vibe and convert nearby customers.

Why it works now

  • Speed beats legacy. We swap agency decks for weekly drops—posters, reels, and emails go live while the idea is still hot.
  • Local data > national averages. We track foot traffic, QR scans, and POS bumps so each campaign gets tuned to your block.
  • Neighborhood-first creative. Every asset is built with your regulars in mind: real photos, language they use, and moments they care about.

What you get (beta = free)

  • Brand and copy refresh tuned to your neighborhood vibe.
  • Window, in-store, and street signage designed for quick print runs.
  • Microsite or landing page with QR-ready funnel and analytics.
  • Weekly co-working sessions to adjust creative and offers in real time.

What we need from you

  • A founder or GM who can green-light copy, offers, and installs quickly.
  • Access to your brand assets, POS data, and any list you want to activate.
  • Agreement to share performance metrics so we can publish before/after results.
  • Feedback on what feels on-brand, what misses, and what you need next.

How the build works

  1. Neighborhood audit: We walk the block, review your touchpoints, and define the vibe to amplify.
  2. Beta build: We design creative, spin up the microsite, and prep print-ready files.
  3. Live activation: Install signage, launch promos, and hook up analytics for the run.
  4. Review & reset: We debrief with metrics, capture lessons, and plan the next drop.

Beta pricing

During beta, Lakeshore Local vibe marketing builds are free in exchange for shared metrics and a public mini case study. When we exit beta, pricing will shift to a flat monthly creative fee plus production costs—beta partners lock in founding rates and a guaranteed launch window each quarter.

Use cases we like

  • Cafés and bakeries introducing seasonal menus or merch drops.
  • Boutique fitness or wellness studios filling new class formats.
  • Neighborhood retailers refreshing window displays and local promos.
  • Service pros—salons, bike repair, florists—launching membership offers.

Proof & transparency

We show the work. Expect shared dashboards for walk-ins, QR scans, list growth, and campaign ROI. Weekly recaps highlight what resonated and what we’re changing next.

You’ll also see anonymized learnings from other beta shops—signage prints, landing page heatmaps, and offer tests that moved the needle.

FAQ

Do we need a big marketing team?

Nope. Most beta partners are owner-operators or lean teams. We bring the design, copy, and lightweight tech—just loop in whoever approves offers and signage.

Who covers printing and media spend?

During beta we’ll design everything and share ready-to-print files. You cover hard costs like printing, boosts, or ad buys so we can move fast without procurement delays.

How long does the beta run?

Each build runs about six weeks from kickoff to recap. After beta you can roll into the paid program with founding rates or take the assets and run your own campaigns.
